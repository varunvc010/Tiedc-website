About Us

About Us

TIEDC works to harness the talents and research strengths available in different Engineering disciplines and apply the same to socially relevant projects in the form of Startup ventures. TIEDC provides mentoring and technological guidance to the perspective Entrepreneurs.

TIEDC is supported by the Department of Industries, Himachal Pradesh under Chief Minister’s Startup/Innovation Projects/New Industries Scheme.

Our works

Entrepreneurship programs have been a challenge in Indian context due to lack of information regarding the business prospects in various technology-driven sectors.

Additionally, there has been lack of awareness regarding the support systems to Startup and Innovative projects from different Government agencies.

Therefore, there is need to create awareness among the youth, looking for opportunities to explore their full potential by setting up their own business ventures.
