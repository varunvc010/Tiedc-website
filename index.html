About Us

TIEDC works to harness the talents and research strengths available in different Engineering disciplines and apply the same to socially relevant projects in the form of Startup ventures. TIEDC provides mentoring and technological guidance to the perspective Entrepreneurs.

Our Events

E-summit

The Entrepreneurship Summit of Jaypee University of Information Technology, Solan Himachal Pradesh is conducted with the vision of molding the future you of the country into self-independent and intellectual beings. From leadership talks to exciting competitions to Informal nights. This E-Summit will be a whirlwind of events exploring the hidden entrepreneur in each of you with the promise of the best time!

Student Entrepreneurship Workshop

The Startup Experience introduces students to the entrepreneurial mindset and provides hands-on training in high impact entrepreneurship. Participants develop creative capacity and acquire the necessary skills to build scalable startups that solve real problems.

Webinars Sessions

Webinars are at TIED cell to provide the students with an opportunity to get a head start in the field of entrepreneurship and marketing. Students get an opportunity to interact with professors and speakers and conclude with a Q&A sessions at the end to answer viewers' questions about the content from the presentation.

TEDxJUIT

Our goal is to bring together bright minds to give talks that are idea-focused and on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning, inspiration and wonder and provoke conversations that matter. We have made it an immediate priority to focus on the stories of Incredible India and its people, projects and ideas. We believe your voice would be critical addition to the TEDx JUIT stage.

R&D Expo

Two main events in R&D Expo 2019 were Hackathon amd Ideathon. Hackathon was s a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others were involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, and project managing. Ideathon was the event concentrating on pitching of an idea on the open stage that marked a significant change in the trend. Participants presented their ideas using posters designed by them.

Starup Challenges

Students are provided with certain problems for which they need to find some solution. The ideas are pitched in front of a panel of esteemed judges. This workshop aims to create a new marketplace to scale up, find new markets, collaborate or find new partners.It provide an exposure of the current demands in the market place.
